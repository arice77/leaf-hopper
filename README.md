# Leaf Hopper

A nature-inspired Web3 casual game where you play as a forest spirit navigating through mystical mazes, collecting light orbs, and avoiding poisonous beetles.

## üéÆ How to Play

- Use **WASD** or **Arrow Keys** to navigate your forest spirit
- Collect light orbs to increase your score
- Avoid poisonous beetles that will end your run
- Progress through increasingly challenging levels
- Connect your MetaMask wallet to save progress and earn rewards
